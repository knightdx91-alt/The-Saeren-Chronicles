Ch.11 reuses it for both transport ("take the train to the slow junction") and post ("by the slow line from the capital," p77) and goods ("came up on the slow line from somewhere warm," p49) — all consistent with the established term.

### [MINOR-002] Age stated as "sixteen years" — verify intent
- **Audit:** Audit 2d (age tracking)
- **Location:** p39 ("the hardest thing she had done in a bed in sixteen years"); p179 ("at sixteen, had decided wholeness was worth more than consent").
- **Description:** Consistent with the RESOLVED age ruling (Bk3 may state she is sixteen). p39 "in sixteen years" = her whole life; p179 "at sixteen" = her age at the rebirth. NOTE: the rebirth was ~1 year ago and she is sixteen NOW, so "at sixteen … decided wholeness" implies she was already ~sixteen at the rebirth a year ago (would make her ~17 now). Mild tension with the "16 now / 15-going-16 at rebirth" timeline, but reads as loose retrospective phrasing ("at sixteen" used loosely for "as a girl of about that age"), not a hard contradiction. Flagged for awareness only.
- **Suggested fix:** Optional — if precision is wanted, p179 could read "at fifteen" or "a year ago" to keep the rebirth firmly one year back. Non-blocking; the on-page age (16 now) is correct.
