- **Description:** The chapter twice walks Viridia's "cold working part" right up to the edge of the Ch.10 revelation that the tears are *pulled toward the source she became*: "water finds a low place because the low place pulls, and... the pull had a direction, and... the direction was not random" — and she stops the thought before it "arrives." This is a WARNING, not CRITICAL, because the text never names the source/her as the destination — the inference is left to the reader and the character refuses it.

- **Suggested fix (optional, author/editor call):** No change required for canon. If the team wants to protect the Ch.10 reveal's force, consider making the withheld thought one shade more opaque here (e.g., keep "the pull had a direction" but drop "not random," so the *directionality* is felt without the *non-randomness* that points at an intelligence/target). Leave as-is if the intended effect is dramatic irony building toward Ch.10.
