# Roadmap ↔ Actual Books (1 & 2) — Reconciliation for Book Three

The Series Roadmap (`series-roadmap-book3.md`) was written **before** Books One and Two were
finalized. Where the finished books diverge from the roadmap, **the finished books win** (they are
upstream canon). This note lists the divergences the architect must honor. Grounded in Book Two's
`ENTITY_STATE.yaml` and the staged Book Two chapters (Ch.18 rebirth, Ch.20 finale).

## MAJOR — must reshape Act One

### 1. Viridia's mana-sight is SPENT at the start, not fully present
- **Roadmap premise says:** "She can feel every core in the world... she can feel the state of the
  world's magic at all times."
- **Books actually ended:** the rebirth (Bk2 Ch.18) **burned her always-on mana-sight to the wick**
  as its named cost (TEMPORARY, author-settled 2026-06-23). End of Bk2 she reads the world by
  *ordinary eyes* and **cannot see the lattice/cores/threads**; it returns **slowly, "an ember at a
  time," over a long count of weeks/months.**
- **Book Three must therefore:** open with the sight **returning**, not omnipresent. Her growing
  ability to feel cores again should be a *measured Act-One re-acquisition* (and the vehicle by which
  she first notices the widened void-rim). She can still **work magic by intent** (Moravian, opening
  seams by meaning it) the whole time — it's the passive SIGHT that guttered, not the gift.

## MODERATE — fix before writing the relevant beats

### 2. Alice ↔ Moravian: direction is REVERSED in the bible
- **Roadmap bible says:** Moravian known by "...Alice (taught by Viridia)..."
- **Book Two Ch.19 actually says:** Alice is "the girl who **taught her** [Viridia] Moravian over a
  desk." → **Alice taught Viridia** (Book One), not the reverse.
- **Honor:** do NOT write Viridia as Alice's Moravian teacher. If anything, the debt runs the other
  way. (Also: Book-Two Alice is **changed** — calm, stilled, warmth banked-not-out, the one crooked
  tooth; recovering slowly. Write her from that state, not the pre-capture Alice.)

### 3. Mrs. Zoran — unconfirmed; do not assume
- The bible lists Mrs. Zoran knowing Moravian ("taught by Viridia") as a live ally. Her
  **survival/whereabouts after Hazel burned (Bk1 Ch.16) are not established** in Books One/Two.
  Treat her presence in Book Three as an OPEN author decision, not a given.

### 4. Age / timeline
- Roadmap: "**months** since the rebirth" and Viridia is "**sixteen**." Book Two ended with her ~**15**
  (turned 15, Ch.12, no number). A few months ≠ 16. **Confirm the Act-One time-skip runs closer to
  ~a year** if she is to be 16 on the page (decision: age MAY be stated — see STATE.yaml).

## LOAD-BEARING CARRY-OVERS (consistent — keep central)

- **The Death symbol CLOSES PORTALS.** Viridia knows it (Drake taught it, Bk2 Ch.8; used on the
  true-glass Ch.10; Drake killed Meros with death-flame Ch.17). Since the Horrors come through
  **portals**, this is the obvious tactical spine of Book Three — but the roadmap's own warning holds:
  the symbol is *easy and clean and asks nothing*, which is exactly its danger. Use it meaningfully,
  not freely.
- **The source's physical location = the Book One cavern** (Bk1 Ch.14 / Bk2 Ch.18): low unworked-stone
  chamber, the stone **pedestal**, the **pool that IS the source**, reached by **portal/seam**. Any
  Book-Three return to the source must portal THERE and match that prose.
- **The void-rim differential** widened "a shade" as the weave ran full (Bk2 Ch.20). **Abstract only —
  NO entity, NO void woman.** This is the physical basis for the roadmap's "pressure differential" and
  the Horrors being *pulled*, not attacking.
- **Bella's-name thread** runs DOWN into the source, warm, unfollowable; touched again at the rebirth
  pedestal (Bk2 Ch.18) and let go. Held for Book Three.
- **Raizen is HUMAN now** (Bk2 Ch.19): a lean dark-haired young man, new to feet/cold/buttons; the
  True-Bond intact (they outlive almost everyone — the roadmap's ending leans on this).
- **Marick / Fen** (RESOLVED 2026-06-24): Marick (Bk2 Ch.20 seed — the candle handed the whole dark
  half, ignores the worn warning, picks up the chalk) is the one who **opens** the bad working/portal;
  **Fen** is his **assistant who runs** at the very end (the roadmap's final-image character). Distinct
  people; Fen is new to Book Three.
- **Dead/gone, do not resurrect:** Jazen (beheaded, Bk2 Ch.16), Erik Meros / High Chancellor (Drake's
  death-flame, Ch.17 — capital leaderless), Lady Lightwell (Bk1, Jazen's mother), Brutus, Hiram,
  Mirelle. Lor-ar RELEASED back to the forest (Bk2 Ch.19; the bond quiet, not broken). Drake & Varissa
  went to the mountain with the expectation of Raizen visiting.
- **Carried objects:** the dark-blue mother's stone + the **Heart of the Spear** (Dangris, Kor-toh).
  Pact-and-Dangris question still open (roadmap).
