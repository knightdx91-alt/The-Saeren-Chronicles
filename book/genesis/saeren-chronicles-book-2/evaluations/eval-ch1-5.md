## PER-CHAPTER NOTES

### Chapter 1 — The Box of Invisible Air · FLOOR 8.5 · PASS
- **Originality 8.5:** Fresh hook for the genre — a refugee whose burden is a *moral* power, established through the camp's quiet economy ("He measured it in winters it would see. He was counting how many of them were left"). One unique element (the consent-engine premise) is more than the commercial bar requires. Held at 8.5 because Ch.1 itself is a re-entry chapter; the structural originality is latent, not yet demonstrated.
- **Theme 9.0:** Planted organically, not stated. The council scene embodies "everyone is right and there is not enough world to keep what everyone loves" — theme as question, with ~35% pure texture (chalk, scorched detail, the baby that may be the same baby).
- **Characters 9.0:** Cover-the-name passes cleanly — Amber's finger-ticking and "There is no three. I had a three. It drowned" is unmistakable; Lor-ar's rationed "little one"; Viridia's single-word "Yes." Secondary characters (Brutus, Mirelle, Mella, Coram, the silver-bun woman, Hiram) each get an independent beat, not prop-function. Chaos present and INHABITED: the banked-thought at the council ("Only her breath changed") is failed-then-recovered control, not narrated analytically.
- **Prose/Voice 9.0:** Close-book candidate: "It was the place where pain would be if he were gone, kept warm and open and waiting." Voice changes under pressure (long deep-water grief sentences → flat "Yes."). One deliberately plain "ugly"/quiet sentence present ("She filed that away, the way she filed everything").
- **Pacing 8.5:** Value shift present (idle → useful → council weight → the line is coming in); strong end-hook. Held at 8.5 because it is a deliberately decelerated re-establishing chapter — appropriate, but not propulsive in the thriller sense the STATE pacing note nudges toward.
- **Emotion 9.0:** Anchor = the slate with the word for *safe* written for children in a box that may not be safe by week's end. Body-rebel near-miss ("the body... pressed... toward release. She pressed back").
- **World 8.5:** The ward-as-"box of invisible air," the painted forest, Moravian taught to six-year-olds — economical, immersive. Held at 8.5: world is re-stated from Book One more than newly expanded here.
- **Discovery Test (Ch.1 of Book Two — applied as series re-entry):** Bookstore Browse: BUY ("On the fourth morning the camp put her to work, which was the kindest thing it could have done" is a strong, voice-forward first line). Amazon Look Inside: BUY for a returning reader; SAMPLE for a cold reader (it assumes Book One). BookTok Pitch: SHAREABLE (the consent-power premise is viral-adjacent but lands fully later). No 2-of-3 negative → no critical opening flag.
- **Tomorrow Test:** ANCHOR EXISTS — IMAGE (the slate; "the word for safe"). Plus QUOTE ("counting how many winters were left").

### Chapter 2 — The Thread Pulled Taut · FLOOR 8.5 · PASS
- **Originality 8.5 / Theme 8.5:** Theme allowed to recede slightly for Raizen's joy (sanctioned by foundation). The parallel structure (reunion vs. the scout) is a genuine craft choice, executed cleanly.
- **Characters 9.0:** Raizen's voice is a NEW and distinct register — comic, imperious, bond-honest ("You feel like a struck bell. Make the face that goes with it"). Ferro, a one-chapter character, gets her own interiority (the cut-loose horse, the swallowed stone) — secondary chaos satisfied. Drake's grief-as-bluntness intact ("Doesn't look like much").
- **Prose/Voice 9.0:** "the place where the burners keep the people they do not burn" — chilling economy. Voice under pressure holds (Viridia's two-word "I heard").
- **Pacing 8.5:** Good cross-cut rhythm; value shift (joy → dread) is the chapter's spine. End-hook strong ("Send it").
- **Emotion 9.0:** Anchor = hope and dread in the same breath, carried by the bond going slack (joy) exactly as the scout climbs the lane (dread). Emotional surprise: the reunion is undercut by comedy, then by horror — expected joy is interrupted.
- **World 8.5:** The "collecting, not killing" mechanic deepens the antagonist's logic intelligently.

### Chapter 3 — Alive Is Not the Same as Safe · FLOOR 8.5 · PASS (strongest emotion in batch)
- **Theme 9.0:** The forbidden thought ("I could end this") is taken out, sized, and stowed — the book's spine made interior without becoming essay.
- **Characters 9.0:** Jazen's Jan monologue is his first full development beat and it lands — "I'm just the thing that empties roads." Mirror to Viridia made explicit but not over-explained. Amber's "the dark always votes yes. Wait for the daylight to vote" is character-specific wisdom.
- **Emotion 9.5:** Highest in the batch. The "calm hands" detail — the wrong detail to undo her, a clerk's note echoing a teacher's praise — is a precise body-rebel-adjacent gut-punch carried entirely by an object/phrase with zero intensity-words. Anchor is unforgettable.
- **Pacing 8.5:** Three-days-that-don't-pass is deliberate; value shift (not-knowing → crueler knowing). Held at 8.5 for the same intentional deceleration.
- **Prose/Voice 9.0:** "relief... like a spring breaking open under a stone she had set on it herself." Underline test clears.

### Chapter 4 — His Mother's Papers · FLOOR 8.5 · PASS
- **Originality 8.5 / Theme 8.5:** Spiral structure (the cache) executed; theme recedes appropriately for community/grief texture.
- **Characters 9.0:** Jazen's open weeping ("He let them") set against Viridia's witnessed restraint is the canon contrast working as character. Mirelle's additive backstory (widow, baby born four months after) lands as texture, not info-dump.
- **Prose/Voice 9.0:** "a face with nothing on it is the loudest face there is to a man who knows the trick." Em-dash count exactly 4 (at the ceiling) — gate-clean but the tightest in the batch.
- **Pacing 8.5:** The reveal cadence (note → family chart → the open circle) is well-spaced; end-hook = a road that is not a sword.
- **Emotion 9.0:** Anchor = the unsigned note in Lightwell's hand — "She had been chosen. First. On purpose. By a woman in the ground who would never know she'd come through." Retroactive recontextualization of Book One delivers strong Devoted-Reader payoff.
- **World 8.5:** Lightwell's seam-reading method, the open-circle sign, the dateless family name (Book-Three re-seed) all planted with restraint.

### Chapter 5 — The Furnace and the Match · FLOOR 8.5 · AVG 9.00 · PASS (strongest overall)
- **Originality 9.0:** The conservation-of-magic law ("You cannot make a thing whole. You can only move where the broken part lives") is a genuine, branded world-rule innovation — the cost goes to the void-rim, not to her. This is the structural subversion the genre rewards.
- **Theme 9.5:** The moral engine made fully concrete: the cure is free to her and catastrophic somewhere she'll never have to stand. "the discipline of restraint, of standing in front of a thing you could fix at no cost you could feel and choosing not to." Theme as unresolved question, embodied.
- **World 9.5:** The lattice, the rowan rebirth-in-miniature, the *not* at the rim handled as ABSTRACT absence (canon-correct — no entity). Sense-of-wonder + dread fused.
- **Characters 8.5:** Lor-ar carries the chapter and is superb ("The day I tell you no is the day I have decided I am wiser than you about your own soul"). Held at 8.5 (the floor-holder) because it is a near-two-hander; Viridia's chaos here is more mediated/observed (the "cold working part" narrates the arithmetic) than inhabited — appropriate to the scene, but it caps Characters short of 9.0.
- **Prose/Voice 9.0:** "spring had been kept in a jar and the jar had broken." Close-book candidate. The opening "There is a lie people tell about hard things" risks Pattern #16 (philosophical aside) but earns it by making it the chapter's literal thesis-under-test, not a coffee-mug truth.
- **Pacing 8.5:** Essayistic→scene structure is the riskiest in the batch; the front philosophical frame is the one place a Devourer could slow. Rescued by the three-word end-hook ("*Who else knows.*") — the sharpest pull in the five chapters.
- **Emotion 9.0:** Anchor = the white-foaming rowan and the hair's-breadth lean of the *not* toward full. Cost carried by objects/observation, no intensity-words.
