## Findings by severity

### CRITICAL — none.

### WARNING (1)
- **W1 — "capitol" vs "capital" spelling inconsistency.** Ch.2 uses **"capitol"** twice in
  Viridia's interior narration (line 37: "when this camp moves on the capitol"; line 83: "The
  resistance would move on the capitol within the week"). Everywhere else in Ch.2 and across
  Ch.3–5 the city is spelled **"capital"** (the intended sense — the seat of government / the city,
  not a domed legislative *building*). This is an internal spelling contradiction for the same
  referent. **Fix:** change both "capitol" → "capital" in Ch.2. WARNING (not minor) only because a
  copyeditor/hostile reader will flag it and it is the same word two ways on the same page-set.

### MINOR (4)
- **M1 — Mella named for the first time.** B1 left the woman-in-blue unnamed; Ch.3 names her
  Mella. Not a contradiction (traits match), but it is a new canon fact — lock it in ENTITY_STATE
  so she stays "Mella." (Recommend adding to `council_recur`.)
- **M2 — Mirelle's widowhood is new backstory.** B1 only had her teaching at the long table; Ch.1
  adds a husband lost in a raid two winters back + a baby born four months after. Additive and
  consistent, not contradictory; log it so later chapters (her Ch.11 death) stay consistent.
- **M3 — Viridia's age phrasing.** Ch.2 line 109 Viridia says "Fifteen soon"; Ch.3 line 97
  narration says "fifteen years old in five days." Not a contradiction ("soon" ⊃ "five days"),
  but the two are a day apart in-story and the precision jumps; harmless. Confirms her birthday
  lands ~the march. No action needed beyond awareness for later age references.
- **M4 — Hiram's surname newly given.** Ch.4 "Hiram Vell." New detail, no prior surname to
  conflict with. Log to ENTITY_STATE so it persists to his Ch.11 death.
