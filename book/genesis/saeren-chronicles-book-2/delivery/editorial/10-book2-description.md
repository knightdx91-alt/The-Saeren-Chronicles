## Full description (IngramSpark / retailer listing)

The war that burned Hazel to the ground has finally found her.

In the smoking aftermath of the school's fall, Viridia escapes to a hidden
resistance camp — and to the one secret she has never dared say aloud. She was
born *whole:* both halves of a magic her world tore cleanly in two six hundred
years ago. And she has come to understand something no one else alive knows. She
could mend the severing. Not for herself. For everyone. All at once — whether they
consent to it or not.

But the girl who once survived by being invisible is done being saved by other
people. Alice — the only person who ever saw the drowning beneath her quiet — is
alive, captured, and held in the heart of the capital that would kill Viridia for
existing. A war she cannot stop marches closer by the day. And the grieving young
leader at her side is walking, clear-eyed, toward his own end.

Viridia will try every bloodless road first. She will spend every day she has
looking for an answer that doesn't ask her to remake the world without its
permission. But the window is closing — and when the two armies finally meet, the
cost of doing the impossible will be counted in the people she loves.

*The Resistance* is the second book of **The Saeren Chronicles**: a quiet,
devastating fantasy about grief, found family, consent, and the unbearable weight
of being the only one who can.
