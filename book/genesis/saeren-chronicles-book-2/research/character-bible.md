HOW TO USE THIS DOCUMENT

This is a living document. Every time you make a decision about a character — a new physical detail, a backstory revelation, a relationship change — update this document immediately. Do not rely on memory. One contradiction breaks reader trust.

When starting a new writing session, paste the entries for every character who appears in that session.

VIRIDIA SAEREN
Main Protagonist — Book One through Three
================================================
AGE: 14 at the start of Book One. Ages through the trilogy.

PHYSICAL DESCRIPTION (decided 2026-05-29):
— Silver-blonde hair, falling to the middle of her back. Worn loose when she has not the energy to braid it.
— Green eyes.
— Slight, fine-boned build. Height 5'5" (she is slight, NOT short — do not write her as small/short).
— Carries herself shy but firm: shoulders drawn slightly inward as though making herself a smaller target, yet with a spine that does not bend. Grows more confident as she settles into a place.
— Grief worn inward and staid. She suffers in silence and NO ONE sees her cry — she masters it where others can see. This restraint is deliberate and load-bearing: it is what will make her eventual breakdown land.
— She wears the dark-blue stone necklace (her mother's), gripping it when stressed or grieving; carved symbols seem to hold a faint light even in sunlight.

CORE PERSONALITY TRAITS:
— Highly intelligent and perceptive. Notices things others miss; reads people through the one detail that doesn't fit.
— Morally serious; acts on conviction even at cost.
— Quiet. Reaches conclusions quickly and keeps them to herself until sure.
— Dry humor that appears rarely; when it does it feels like a gift.
— Does not show off. Competence reveals itself through action.
— Grief-shaped. Fills herself with learning and connection because she fears the emptiness.

WHAT SHE WANTS: To understand what she is. To protect the people she cares about. To make sense of a world that took her parents.

WHAT SHE IS AFRAID OF: Losing more people. Being defined by what she is rather than who she is. Becoming something that uses power the way her enemies do.

MAGICAL NATURE: Both sides of the gift — light and dark — merged into a single unified core, triple the size of any she has encountered. After bonding Raizen her magic runs through her whole body. After touching the source of all magic she becomes the living vessel of the source. Mana sight. Knows the ancient Moravian language.

FOCUS: A silver rod inherited from her mother's side, predating the current magical era. Moravian inscription: "Knowledge is power, yet may whomever hold this rod, have the wisdom to use such power wisely." Mrs. Zoran primed it to a walking-cane length; split orb on one end. Viridia rarely uses it after learning the language.

BONDS:
— True Bond with Raizen (dragon). Life forces tied; equal lifespans; she received his racial memories and innate understanding of magic; he received her human memories.
— Bond with Lor-ar (sylvan tiger, spirit walker). Released at the end of Book Two.

RELATIONSHIPS:
— Alice Pembrook: first genuine friend; grounds her humanity.
— Amber Summers: mentor who becomes peer.
— Lor-ar: teacher and protector.
— Raizen: partner and mirror.
— Lady Lightwell: complicated authority figure.
— Mrs. Zoran: first teacher who sees her potential.

UNRESOLVED MYSTERIES ABOUT HER:
— What did her parents actually die from? (AUTHORIAL TRUTH now set — see PARENTS' DEATH below — but Viridia does not yet know, so it remains a reader mystery to be revealed later.)
— Who wrote the note that came with the focus? (Still open.)
— What did she see in the well of magic that connected to her mother? (Still open.)

ALICE PEMBROOK
Supporting Protagonist — Books One through Three
================================================
NAME: Spelling resolved 2026-05-29 to PEMBROOK (use everywhere; the draft's "Pembreok" is retired).
AGE: 14 at the start of Book One.

PHYSICAL DESCRIPTION:
— Long blond hair
— Green eyes
— Wide friendly smile showing slightly crooked teeth (one turned just out of line)
— Smattering of freckles on her nose
— A bit on the heavier side
— Wears the Hazel uniform: black vest with white H over the right breast, white undershirt, dark brown skirt to above the knee, long white socks, black shoes

CORE PERSONALITY TRAITS:
— Warm and immediately friendly; the kind of person who moves seats to make a connection.
— First-generation mage — not from a magical family, quietly self-conscious about it (waves it off rather than dwelling).
— Practical and grounded where Viridia is philosophical.
— Experiences joy fully and without embarrassment.
— More politically aware than she lets on; knew dark-mage families in her building.
— Medical inclination; helped care for her grandfather; not squeamish.

WHAT SHE WANTS: To belong. To be taken seriously despite her background. To help people.
WHAT SHE IS AFRAID OF: Being looked down on as first-generation. Being away from family for four years.

MAGICAL NATURE: Standard one-sided light magic. Viridia teaches her the Moravian language in nightly lessons; she stops using her star focus once she knows enough symbols, except in class.

FOCUS: A star that pulses with inner light on a small chain bracelet; chose it on instinct.

SPONSOR AT SCHOOL: A fourth-year who is herself first-generation; steers Alice toward the medical wing.

FATE: [STILL TO BE DECIDED — most urgent decision before revising Book One.] Alice disappears during the school attack in Chapter 16.

RELATIONSHIP TO VIRIDIA: Her mirror — ordinary where Viridia is extraordinary, warm where Viridia is guarded. The first person who asks if Viridia is okay and does not push when she says yes (established on the page, Ch.1 Scene 6).

AMBER SUMMERS
Supporting Character — Books One and Two, minor Book Three
================================================
AGE: 17–18; a fourth-year.
PHYSICAL DESCRIPTION:
— Long black hair in a ponytail
— Black nail polish (chipped)
— Wears a light-core necklace (artificial, provided by Lady Lightwell) — appears as a thin chain with a pale stone
— Also wears a hidden necklace with some of the same symbols as Viridia's — sustains her after her light core was shattered
CORE PERSONALITY TRAITS: Impulsive (sponsored Viridia within hours); easy-going manner masking real damage; loyal to a cost; genuinely perceptive (recognized Viridia's necklace immediately); dark humor.
BACKSTORY: At eight, her parents were killed in a robbery — mother struck in the kitchen; father (recently injured at the shoe factory, missing a leg) fought bare-handed and bled out. Amber hid, came down to find them dead, and instinctively shattered her light core trying to bring them back — succeeding only long enough to say goodbye. A dark-mage neighbor knew Lady Lightwell, who provided the sustaining necklace.
WHAT SHE WANTS: To give others the chance she was given.
WHAT SHE IS AFRAID OF: Being defined by what she lost; watching someone else lose the same and being unable to help.
MAGICAL NATURE: Shattered light core sustained via the necklace; dark core used instinctively that night; routes through the light-core necklace; has never summoned a creature companion through it (until the iron boar).
THE IRON BOAR: Bonded companion; dissolves into a white charm on her bracelet.
RELATIONSHIP TO VIRIDIA: First adult-adjacent person to see Viridia as an equal; becomes peer; in Book Two challenges Viridia on the ethics of the magical rebirth.
SECRET SHE KEEPS: Lady Lightwell is Jazen's mother. Amber knows; she does not tell Viridia.

LOR-AR
Major Supporting Character — Books One and Two
================================================
AGE: Three hundred years (middle-aged for a sylvan tiger; spirit walkers can near five hundred). "-ar" is his earned spirit-walker title; "Lor" is his name.
PHYSICAL: Seven feet nose-to-tail at full size; fur sky-blue and forest-green; bone-white horns veined with blue; saber-like fangs; huge paws; lightning crackling when alert; can shrink to house-cat size; lightning-blue eyes with bolts in the irises.
TRAITS: Three centuries of patience; finds humans fascinating and exhausting; affection expressed through honesty not warmth; dry wit. Uses "little one" with Viridia — REDUCE in later chapters; save for moments that matter.
NATURE: Spirit walker (racial/ancestral memory); both sides of the gift; demonstrates twin flames (black + white combined); sees cores without coating his eyes.
BOND: Genuine summoned bond (not a True Bond); Viridia can and does release him at the end of Book Two; painful for both. Last words: "I will not forget you little one."
ABILITIES: Shrink/grow; static lock for a rider; always-on mana sight; spirit walking; lethal, efficient combat when Viridia is threatened.

Secondary Character — Book Two
================================================
AGE: Mid-twenties.

TRAITS: Purposeful; grief-shaped (his sister Jan killed in a government raid; he lost control and killed innocents, carries the guilt); sued for peace repeatedly and was refused; declares war only after the final refusal; quick to recognize capability.
