# Series Order — Author Decisions (2026-06-22)

Settled by the author. Binding for the whole Ne'veran universe.

## Publication order (reader-facing) — CONFIRMED, follows the bible
1. **Land of the Guardians**  (most accessible on-ramp)
2. **The Dragon Isle**  (potential Vol. 2 of Guardians)
3. **Son of None**  (the finale — final reckoning with Alten)
