## 0. Verdict & strategy (why round 4 exists)

**Current status (agent/publisher lens):** strong, much-improved draft; **not yet submission-ready.**
"Promising but needs one more full pass." Heart, voice, magic system, moral complexity, series
potential are all real assets. The blockers are: **early-act pacing (Ch.1–6)**, residual show-vs-tell
and motif repetition, and **genre differentiation** in the crucial first 3 chapters (the sample most
agents request).

**Round-4 thesis:** one focused pass that (1) sharpens the **first 50 pages** so an agent doesn't stop,
(2) fixes the **middle-act sag** with a war-clock, and (3) finishes the show-don't-tell / motif sweep.
**Weight effort heaviest on Ch.1–7.**

**Pathway read:** Big-5/top agency = unlikely without this pass. Small press/hybrid = possible.
Self-pub = viable *after* this pass + pro cover + fresh betas.

  no NEW cross-chapter repeated 4–6-word phrase, tics under ceiling).
- **Word floors:** total ≥ 85,000 (currently **86,726**, ~1,700 headroom); per-chapter ~4,500. Chapters
  running lean — **Ch.4 (4,475), Ch.6 (4,306)** — must be **converted in place**, not net-cut.
- **Canon guardrails:** Pembrook; JAZEN (not Jasen); rose-tinted spectacles; Viridia silver-blonde/green/5'5",
  no one sees her cry; trains & motorcars; no Hazel houses; no Johnathan Masters / void woman. Keep the
  deliberate motifs (water; bell/gate tie-back; "calm hands"; "edge of the day"; "watch the body"; the
  paired "held breath is empty" absence-echo; the "no one sees you do it → you decide who sees" refrain).
- **Method:** per-chapter — read → fix → gate → check floor → commit. Diffs are the record.

of a clock the reader already knows is running. None require touching Ch.16–18.
