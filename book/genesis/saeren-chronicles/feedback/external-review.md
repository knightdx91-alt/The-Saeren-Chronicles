## Global Fixes (apply everywhere)
- **Pacing:** Early chapters (1–6) are introspection-heavy; tighten 15–25% of
  reflective passages and add micro-active scenes.
- **Show vs. Tell:** Strong overall, but trim occasional summaries (e.g.,
  "she was proud in the small cold way").
- **Sentence Rhythm:** Vary long, flowing sentences — especially in action/dialogue —
  for better propulsion.
- **Repetition:** Motifs enrich, but vary phrasing to avoid fatigue.
- **Transitions:** Smooth school-to-war shifts in late chapters.
- **Technical:** Very clean; minor formatting/typos in raw files. Proof the full compile.
