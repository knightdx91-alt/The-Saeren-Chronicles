# Beta-Reader Package — The Saeren Chronicles, Book One

Developmental reader feedback for the author. Current as of the round-3 manuscript (2026-06-21).

## Contents
| File | What it is |
|------|-----------|
| `beta-reader-report.md` | Five-persona beta panel (Devourer, Critic, Hostile, Casual, Devoted), a synthesis, a "resolved since last panel" tracker, and a prioritized developmental fix list with scores. |

A prior panel (run before the round-2/round-3 revisions) is preserved at
`../../evaluations/review/beta-reader-panel.md` for comparison. Several of its top issues — the
climax causal hole, a thin antagonist, Alice's pre-climax agency, the burned family-tree mystery —
have since been addressed in the manuscript; this report tracks exactly what changed.

## How to read it
- Nothing here blocks submission. The editorial package (`../editorial/`) is the submission-ready set.
- The **"Still standing"** list in the synthesis is the developmental to-do, in priority order. The
  single highest-leverage item is **middle-act pacing (Ch.10–12)** — give the war a clock in the
  middle, not just on the horizon.

## Method note
Personas are the standard five tuned to YA epic/school fantasy (no project-specific `reader-personas.md`
exists). Reactions are grounded in the current text; scores are relative to the prior panel.
