Three days after her parents die, Viridia Saeren walks through the gates of Hazel, the country's most prestigious school for mages, determined that no one will ever see her grieve. In a world that long ago tore its magic in two, she was born whole, both halves, the one thing Hazel exists to make certain can never be, and to be known is to be killed. So she makes herself small, learns in secret what she truly is, and lets in one friend. Then the people who fear what the school hides set out to burn it down, and a girl who survives by disappearing must choose between the many she can save and the one she cannot bear to lose. Hazel Academy is the first book of The Saeren Chronicles, a quiet, lyrical fantasy about grief, found family, and a girl who refuses to be made small.
