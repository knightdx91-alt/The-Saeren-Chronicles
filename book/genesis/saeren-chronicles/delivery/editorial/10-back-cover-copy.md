# Back-Cover Copy & Listing Description

*The Saeren Chronicles — Book One: Hazel Academy* · by Post Peleos
For the printed back cover, and for the IngramSpark / retailer "description" field.

---

## Back-cover blurb (printed on the cover)

> **The last ordinary morning of Viridia's life smelled like scorched bread and her
> mother's tea.**
>
> Three days later she walks through the gates of Hazel — the most prestigious school
> for mages in the country — orphaned, silent, and certain of one thing: no one is
> ever going to see her grieve.
>
> Viridia is good at not being seen. She is about to learn why it has kept her alive.
>
> In a world that long ago tore its magic cleanly in two — light from dark, safe from
> forbidden — Viridia was born *whole.* Both halves. The one thing Hazel was built to
> make certain could never exist. To be known is to be killed. So she makes herself
> small. She learns, in secret, what she truly is. And she lets in one person: Alice,
> the only girl at Hazel who sees the drowning underneath the quiet.
>
> Then the people who fear what this school hides decide to burn it down — and a girl
> who survives by disappearing must choose between the many she can save and the one
> she cannot bear to lose.
>
> A quiet, lyrical fantasy about grief, found family, and a girl who refuses to be made
> small — the first book of **The Saeren Chronicles.**

*(Length is sized for a 6×9 back cover above the barcode zone. If the designer needs it
shorter, cut the fourth paragraph's middle sentences first.)*

## One-line hook (for spine-adjacent blurb / ads / top of back cover)

> She was born with the one magic her world kills to keep buried. Staying invisible
> kept her alive — until the night it cost her the only person who ever saw her.

### Notes for whoever sets up the listing
- The **first ~150 characters** must hook before the retailer "read more" cut: the
  scorched-bread line + the orphaned/silent/"no one sees her grieve" promise do that.
- This description doubles as the IngramSpark **annotation/description** field — mind the
  field's byte limit; the version in `06-metadata-keywords.md` is a tighter alternate.
- Keep the title rendered as **Hazel Academy** (series: *The Saeren Chronicles*), never the
  retired "The Hazel Years."
