## PACING CHECK (required gate)
- **Scene-vs-summary balance:** Healthy. Roughly half immersive scene (drawer, sink, the laugh), half reflective summary (the "next season" backstory, the culture meditation). No sag — each summary stretch is short and pivots on a value shift. No rush; the climax (map) is given room.
- **Dialogue share:** ~0% live dialogue. **Below the Romance 30-45% band — and that is correct and acceptable here.** This is a deliberately interior single-location grief chapter; the next chapter (Ch.2, dialogue-heavy two-hander) restores the band. Flag noted, not penalized.
- **Emotional slot (the "W"):** Correctly the grief-LOW establishing beat. It does not stack same-energy against neighbors (Ch.2 is brisk/object-forward). Right beat for slot 1.
- **Hook-and-pull:** Opening hook (the first line) is excellent. Closing pull (the map + *Click.*) drives hard toward Ch.2. Both present and strong.
- **Length:** 4,906 words vs 5,300 target / 4,800 floor. **Above floor, ~7% under target.** Acceptable; the chapter is tight rather than thin. Optional: ~200-400 words could be banked in the study-sorting movement if the book later runs short against the 220k floor, but do NOT pad — the leanness serves the grief.

**PACING VERDICT: PASS.** Correct low/establishing beat; clean scene/summary balance; strong hook + pull; deliberately (and acceptably) low dialogue; length above floor. No pacing fix required.
