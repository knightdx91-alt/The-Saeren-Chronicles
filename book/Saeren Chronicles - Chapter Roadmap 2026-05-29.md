A NOTE ON CHAPTER BREAKS
================================================
End a chapter when it (1) answers a held question but opens a new one, (2) changes something irreversibly, or (3) leaves a specific image/feeling to sit with. Avoid ending mid-action or after everything is resolved. If, finishing a scene, you ask "what does the reader still want to find out?" and the answer is nothing, the chapter has run too long.
